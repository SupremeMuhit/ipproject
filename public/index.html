ABOUT FLUPPY SNAKE

A retro-futuristic snake game built for pure arcade fun. Experience the evolution of a classic with modern professional themes and challenging modes.

HOW TO PLAY

Controls: Use WASD or Arrow Keys on PC. Swipe or use the D-Pad on mobile.

Objective: Eat food (colored dots) to grow and earn points. Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail.

Modes: Explore different modes like Speed, Survival, and Zen for unique challenges.
